The Current Challenge

  • Connectivity Barriers: Remote farms often lack reliable internet access, making cloud-only solutions impossible.
  • Manual Paperwork: Hand-written forms result in redundant data entry, illegible text, and physical storage costs.
  • Processing Delays: Transporting physical papers back to the office delays encoding, policy issuance, and claims.
  • Calculation Errors: Manual computation of hectares, rates, and livestock coverage leads to inconsistencies.

The Solution

An intelligent, Offline-First web application that travels with the underwriter.
